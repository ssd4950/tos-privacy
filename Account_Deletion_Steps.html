Account & Data Deletion – Ume

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can do so directly within our app.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps inside the Ume app:

  • Open the app and log in to your account.
  • Go to Profile then Settings/Stats.
  • Select Delete Account.
  • Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once you confirm account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted from our systems:

  • User profile details (name, email, contact number, age, location)
  • Uploaded media (photos)
  • Chat history or messages
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Some non-personal, anonymized data (e.g., aggregated analytics) may be retained for security or legal compliance, but this data is not linked to your identity.

Deletion Timeline

Account deletion is processed instantly from the app. In some cases, complete data removal from our backups or logs may take up to 7 days.
